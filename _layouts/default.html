The Will of God

This preview provides a brief outline of the study’s direction and emphasis.

Introduction: Why the common idea of “finding God’s will” deserves closer examination.

Paul’s Language: How Paul speaks about God’s will, purpose, and intention.

Key Terms: A closer look at words often flattened in translation.

Doctrinal Implications: What changes when Scripture is allowed to define the subject on its own terms.

Conclusion: A return to the wording of the text rather than inherited assumptions.
